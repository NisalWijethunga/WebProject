Fostering ocean stewardship through heart-warming connections

The mission of ML Conservancy is to engage, educate and empower people through hands-on
(and virtual)marine science education to foster a connection to the ocean
and to launch long-term environmental stewardship. The points of the compass direct ML Conservancy
to achieve four SDG guiding principles.

 Why turtles? 
      
According to The IUCN Red List of Threatened Species, sea turtle classified as “Endangered”.There are conservation organizations that work to protect sea turtle hatchlings all across the world. Our funds go towards paying local centers to patrol important turtle nesting beaches, protecting turtles that come up to nest, and ensuring that the eggs are protected. On average, we have helped save 10 hatchlings for every $1 donated.
 Where Do The Donations Go? 
      
This funding goes to community-based organizations & voulanteers to Clean much polluted beaches, Running an awareness campaign simultaneously to educate the public about plastic pollution, Segregating plastic to be weighed for future research.In addition, the funds are used to build and maintain hatcheries where the eggs are protected until they hatch and are released to the ocean. We prioritize nesting beach projects that protect the most endangered turtle species and populations, organizations with less stable sources of funding.

Impact!

Donations help us rehabilitate sick animals, build and maintain the much needed infrastructure to save marine life, cover the costs of hiring local residents to patrol turtle nesting beaches and protecting the turtles and their nests. and research & answer questions about the health of our marine environment and teach the public more about our oceans.
