MARINE CONSERVATION

TARGET 01

MARIENE POLLUTION

One of the greatest threats to our oceans is marine pollution (also known as man-made pollution). It's a combination of chemicals and trash, mostly from land, such as discarded plastic and other residential waste, and industrial chemicals that are washed or blown into the ocean, with devastating consequences for marine life and the habitats on which they depend.

MARINE POLLUTION

How we can prevent it?

The only way we can prevent pollution is activities that helps to reduce the poluted areas and hosting awareness programms.Thats why we are trying to calling volunteers for hosting beach clean up campaigns all around the sri lanka.As for the awareness progarm that provides of poaching regulations the initiative also finances repairs and enhancements of critical infrastructure, such as a jetty, turtle tanks, accommodations to volunteers and hatchery centers, to allow volunteers activities by your donations.
