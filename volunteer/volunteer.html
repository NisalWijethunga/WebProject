MARIENE POLLUTION

One of the greatest threats to our oceans is marine pollution (also known as man-made pollution). It's a combination of chemicals and trash, mostly from land, such as discarded plastic and other residential waste, and industrial chemicals that are washed or blown into the ocean, with devastating consequences for marine life and the habitats on which they depend.
